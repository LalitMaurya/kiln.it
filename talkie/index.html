A Talkie combines the narrative power of video and audio with the interactive power of the web. The Talkie toolkit, now open source, makes it easy bring existing webpages and apps to life â€“ or to create interactive animations, presentations and videos from scratch.

Add a voiceover

Talkie makes it easy to add a guided tour or introduction to your webpage, interactive or data visualisation. This helps it tell its story better, converting a simple page or app into an engaging pseudo video.

Empower video

Turning a video into a Talkie enables it to control the whole website that sits around it – or introduce interactive elements over the video itself.
